MONOCHROME BOOK/GIRLS HELP GIRLSL

I found that women are more vulnerable to malice from all aspects in the society since ancient times, especially women are more vulnerable to malice from the same sex. There is more misunderstanding and hurt between the same sex, especially there is misunderstanding and generation gap between women of different generations. In order to help women in the society of relatively disadvantaged groups to reduce some misunderstandings and injuries, as well as the lack of understanding between the same sex, in particular, to make women of different ages and different identities to be more tolerant and considerate of each other. Reflecting on oneself as a woman, we should also help and unite with each other more. Women play different roles in life. They may be grandmothers, mothers or daughters, so we should trust and understand each other more, and choose to reconcile with others and ourselves. After a series of investigations, based on the views of Freud, I used stop-motion animation as the form of expression to show the disputes between three generations of women and the final reconciliation playfully.

PLASTIC POLLUTION

It is mainly through the use of plastic recycled materials to inject secondary life into discarded plastic products, and plastic bottles are used to form the main device, so as to show the new vitality of "waste materials" and convey the idea that "no waste" is beauty. In combination with some new media technologies, the ocean video screen is projected onto the wall, echoing with the main device, enhancing the atmosphere of the entire device space. With the combination of sound, light and electricity, it creates an atmosphere of deep-sea suffocation.
